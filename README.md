Overview

This project is an end-to-end Data Analytics portfolio project focused on analyzing customer shopping behavior and revenue patterns. It demonstrates real-world analytics skills using Python, SQL, Power BI, and storytelling through insights and visuals to support business decision-making.

Dataset

The dataset contains customer demographics, purchase details, product categories, shipping types, seasonal sales, reviews, and subscription status. It is used to analyze revenue drivers, customer behavior, and sales performance across multiple dimensions.

Dashboard

The Power BI dashboard includes:
Total revenue, average purchase value & customer count
Revenue by category, age group, season, and shipping type
Customer segmentation (VIP & repeat customers)
Interactive filters for gender, location, subscription status

Key Insights

Clothing generates the highest revenue
Senior and adult age groups contribute most to total sales
Free shipping leads to higher purchase volume
Repeat and VIP customers significantly impact revenue

Results

The project provides actionable insights into customer behavior, identifies high-value segments, and highlights opportunities to optimize product strategy, marketing, and shipping methods.
